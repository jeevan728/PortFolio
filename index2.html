Comaparative Analysis of wireless Adhoc routing protocols.

Comparision of AODV, DSDV, DSR routing protocols.

Results obtained




Conclusion

• Packet Delivery Ratio, Average Throughput, Average Jitter is high in AODV followed by DSR and then DSDV even when number of nodes are increased.

• Even when speed of mobile nodes is varied(increased), Packet Delivery Ratio, Average Throughput is high in AODV followed by DSR and then DSDV.

• Residual Energy is also high in AODV followed by DSDV and then DSR.

AODV performance is best compared with other two routing protocols because of its ability connection by frequent exchange of data.The simulation shows that AODV pefoems well when number of nodes are hifg and DSR performs good in less dense network. Average End-to-End Delay is very low in DSDV and there is no any change even if no.of nodes are increased .Thus ,it can be concluded that AODV is a very good choice for MANETs.
